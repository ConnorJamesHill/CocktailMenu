Margarita

üçπ
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz Triple sec
  • Salt for rim
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rub lime wedge around the rim of the glass and dip in salt
  2. Add tequila, lime juice, and triple sec to a shaker with ice
  3. Shake well for 15 seconds
  4. Strain into the prepared glass over fresh ice
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz White rum
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 6-8 Fresh mint leaves
  • Club soda

Instructions:

  1. Muddle mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in a glass
  2. Fill glass with ice
  3. Add rum and stir
  4. Top with club soda
  5. Garnish with mint sprig and lime wheel

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Rye whiskey
  • 1 oz Sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Luxardo cherry for garnish

Instructions:

  4. Garnish with a cherry

Whiskey Sour

üçã
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 0.75 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple syrup
  • 1 Egg white (optional)
  • Angostura bitters for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Add bourbon, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to shaker
  2. Dry shake (without ice) for 10 seconds
  3. Add ice and shake vigorously for 15 seconds
  4. Strain into rocks glass over fresh ice
  5. Garnish with 2-3 drops of bitters on foam
